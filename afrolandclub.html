Afroland Club

The Afroland club is a holders only club that will host annual NFT oriented events. The club will provide our holders with a trading utility that educates them on how to better exploit the financial and crypto markets.

Club members will have access to our exclusive parties on exotic resorts all around Africa, creating one in a lifetime experiences and occasional gift boxes containing free merchandise and many amazing souvenirs will be sent to randomly selected holders from time to time.

Afroland is beyond NFT, we are also focused on wealth creation and growth that’s why we hope to provide our club members with an opportunity to own real estate on the metaverse.

At full potential the Afroland club will redefine the lives of our holders, making them smarter, richer and happier.
