ALUTA SWORDSMEN


Effortlessly wielding his powerful blade as though it were an extension of his arm. The Aluta clan rules the north with amazing swordsmanship, and they can easily defeat any foe thanks to their ever-evolving war strategies. According to some, both their strength and frailty come from their golden armlet.


FIKI ARCHERS


Do not be deceived by their flawless curves and angel-like beauty. The predominantly female clan controls the eastern region with the unseen. Like the Ancient god Apollo, the Fiki women send arrows flying with so much precision and they never miss a target, just like the Goddess Astraea. Hitting targets straight from the shadows, catching their enemies completely off guard has earned them the title of the Invisible Maidens Of Death.

The Londe maidens' relationship with their ancestors' spirits serves as their guide.

BACKGROUND STORY

All Four Clans Co-Existed Peacefully For Thousands And Thousands Of Years, Multiplying, Growing, And Prospering. Until One Day, When The "SLAVE RAIDERS" Attacked. They Attacked During The (OZUMBA) Months-A Sixty-Day Power Seeking Ritual. The Fiercest Of The Fiercest, The Strongest Of The Strongest In All Four Clans Travel Far Across The Seven (7) Mountains Of The East And The Rough Tides Of The TAMBO River To Where The Sun Meets The Horizon, Leaving The Children And The Aged Guarded By The Young Soldiers. Attacking Either Of The Four Clans Was Considered A Suicide Attempt, So No One, Not Even The Gods, Foresaw The Coming Of The Fire Spitting Barrel Wielding Demons Who Kidnapped The Children, Raided And Destroyed The Clans. On Hearing The Devastating News, The Four Clans, Led By Their Fiercest 160 Commanders (40 Commanders) In Each Clan, Gathered And Trained The Bravest 15,000 Warriors To Rescue The Children And Seek Vengeance. They chanted, "BLOOD MUST FLOW."




SLAVE RAIDERS


Pale Lanky Men From Far Beyond The Horizons Invaded And Raided The Clans, Killing And Abducting Hundreds Of Children With Technology And Weapons The Clans Had never seen. During the retaliatory battle, thousands of clans fell to their fire-spitting barrels.Only Time Will Tell If The Slave Raiders And Their Vicious Weapons Will Conquer The Combined Might Of The Clans.
