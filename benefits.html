OUR BENEFITS


OWNERSHIP OF THE UNDERLYING ARTWORK.



Each holder would get a free access to their artwork and be able to claim ownership.

EXCLUSIVE ACCESS TO P2E GAME.



Holders would be able to use their NFT as custom made avatars for access to our play to earn games. That is they gain access to the P2E game through their NFT.

HOLDERS ONLY RAFFLES AND DISCOUNT.



Holders would be able to get discounts in merchandise and also take a part in giveaways like airdrops, contests, trivia, e.t.c

INVITE TO EXCLUSIVE ANNUAL PARTIES AND PRIVATE CLUB EVENTS.



AFROLAND PRIVATE CLUB will be hosting exclusive annual parties and private club events for members of our community.

ACCESS TO EXCLUSIVE DISCORD CHANNEL.



Holders would be given roles and special access to a private channel where decisions and contributions that they feel will benefit the community will be acknowledged.
